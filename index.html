About

I am a second-year Master student in Vision & Data Intelligence Center, ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Ye Shi and Prof. Jingya Wang. I hold a B.E. degree from China University of Petroleum, advised by Prof. Yunquan Song.


Currently, I'm a visiting student at Tsinghua University advised by Prof. Yebin Liu on hand-object interaction and manipulation. My research interests span 3D Computer Vision, Embodied AI, and Multimodal Learning.


I actively seek collaborations with researchers sharing these interests. Feel free to contact me for any research collaborations or discussions!
