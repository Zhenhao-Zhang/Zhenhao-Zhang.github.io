I am currently a Second-year Master student in Vision&Data Intelligence Center, ShanghaiTech University under the supervison of Prof. Ye Shi and Prof. Jingya Wang. I received B.E. degree from Department of Computer Science, China University of Petroleum under the supervison of Prof. Yunquan Song.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Multimodal Large Language Models and Embodied AI.
